Feature 01 · Nudging

Nudging keeps drivers where the orders already are

Before a zone gets busy, the marketplace nudges nearby drivers toward it with a small, visible earnings incentive — the same mechanic that lets platforms cut driver downtime by reducing wait time between jobs. Less deadhead distance, more paid minutes per hour, and drivers stay inside the delivery radius fleet operators actually need covered.

Reducing the gap between rides is core to how modern dispatch platforms lift driver earning time — see Uber's own account of how proximity-aware matching increases business for drivers: Uber, Marketplace Matching ↗

Feature 02 · Demand planning

Demand planning ends the guesswork of how many drivers to call up

The marketplace forecasts order volume by zone and hour, then tells fleet managers exactly how many drivers to schedule — before the queue builds, not after. That solves the two costliest states in delivery ops at once: idle bikes burning fixed cost, and missed SLAs from being short-staffed at 5pm.

Academic work on gig marketplaces shows financial incentives calibrated to real-time demand can lift service capacity meaningfully without added cost — the same principle behind ScootHero's demand planning. Wharton, Behavioral & Economic Drivers of Gig Workers ↗

Feature 03 · Sourcing

Finding drivers becomes a post-and-fill, not a recruitment drive

Post a shift or a zone and the marketplace matches it against a live, vetted driver pool — no job ads, no walk-in interviews, no dead time between "we need a driver" and "a driver is riding." For rental operators this means an idle bike gets a renter fast; for fleets it means peak season doesn't require a hiring sprint.

South Africa's e-commerce and parcel volumes are climbing fast enough that fleet operators are expanding capacity continuously — the bottleneck is matching supply to that growth, not a shortage of would-be riders. Business Market Insights, Africa Last-Mile Delivery Market ↗

Feature 04 · Admin & compliance

Paperwork stops being a full-time job for your fleet manager

Licences, PrDP status, roadworthy checks and POPIA-compliant driver contracts live in one place, with automatic expiry reminders before a document — or a driver — quietly goes non-compliant. Onboarding a new driver is a digital form, not a folder.

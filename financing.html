Financing

From outright purchase to full maintenance lease — a path for every balance sheet.

Financing, via our Eqstra partnership

Own outright, rent-to-own, or lease — matched to how you actually run cash

ScootHero's fleet financing runs through our Eqstra partnership, giving every fleet type a path that fits its balance sheet instead of forcing one model on everyone.

Outright purchase

Best for: self-run fleets with capital to deploy
  • Full asset ownership, no ongoing payments
  • Maximum long-term ROI per bike
  • Sale-and-leaseback available later to unlock capital

Rent-to-buy / Lease-to-own

Best for: rental operators building an owned fleet
  • Start with manageable weekly payments
  • Build equity in the bikes you rent out
  • Lower upfront investment than buying outright

Full maintenance lease

Best for: corporate fleets protecting cash flow
  • Preserve working capital, off balance sheet
  • Maintenance, tyres and servicing bundled in
  • Predictable monthly cost, IFRS16-aligned reporting

Structure and terms informed by Eqstra's full-maintenance leasing model — Eqstra, The Uptick of Fleet Leasing in SA ↗ and Eqstra, Leasing vs Buying Fleet Vehicles ↗.
